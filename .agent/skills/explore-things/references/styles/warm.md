# Warm Style

Cream background, rounded corners, and a focus on a "human/friendly" feel for daily life topics.

## Design Aesthetic

Soft, inviting, and emotionally resonant. This style focuses on the human experience of science and technology. It uses rounded shapes and warm tones to reduce the "coldness" of technical topics.

## Background

- Color: Warm Cream or Almond (#FFF8E7)
- Texture: Soft, paper-like feel

## Visual Elements

- Rounded corners on all objects and containers
- Soft, diffused lighting
- Subdued, warm color palette (oranges, soft reds, warm browns)
- Focus on "human" scales and perspectives
- Friendly, handwritten-style annotations

## Style Rules

### Do

- Prioritize warmth and approachability
- Use rounded, "safe" shapes
- Maintain a gentle, comforting tone
- Focus on the "human" aspect of the topic
- Use soft, low-contrast shadows

### Don't

- Use sharp angles or aggressive lines
- Use cold, harsh colors (like bright blue or neon green)
- Use clinical or industrial-looking backgrounds
- Make the subject feel too distant or abstract

## Best For

Psychology, wellness, everyday science, biology of the home, and any topic where a friendly, human-centric approach is beneficial.
