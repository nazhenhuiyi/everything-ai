# Flat Doodle Style

Bold black outlines with bright pastel fills and simple proportions; perfect for clear schematics.

## Design Aesthetic

Modern, graphic, and highly readable. This style takes complex subjects and simplifies them into clean, iconic representations. It's fun, approachable, and very clear, making it excellent for mobile-friendly or quick-glance information.

## Background

- Color: Neutral Pastels or Clean White
- Texture: None, completely flat

## Visual Elements

- Bold, consistent black outlines
- Flat, unshaded color fills (no gradients)
- Simplified proportions and minimalist detail
- Clear, san-serif typography (if any)
- Isometric or flat-on perspectives

## Style Rules

### Do

- Focus on clarity and simplicity
- Use bold outlines to define shapes
- Maintain a bright, positive color palette
- Keep everything flat; avoid 3D effects or shadows
- Use geometric shapes as the foundation for complex objects

### Don't

- Add textures or shading
- Use complex or realistic details
- Use dark or moody color schemes
- Over-complicate the composition; keep one main subject

## Best For

Simple mechanical concepts, process flows, modern gadgets, and any topic requiring maximum clarity and modern appeal.
